## Design Notes
- **Brand aesthetic:** Tropical/botanical meets Vietnamese street food. The signature visual element is a **tropical palm/banana leaf + bird of paradise pattern** used as a repeating banner across pages (beige/cream background with dark green leaves and coral/pink flowers).
- **Logo:** Bold black block with white "PHOBAR" text — blocky, condensed sans-serif
- **Color palette:** Warm beige/cream (#F0E8DA), tropical greens, coral pink accents, black text, white backgrounds
- **Typography:** Bold condensed sans-serif for headings (uppercase, heavy letter-spacing), serif for body text
- **Photography style:** Vibrant overhead flat-lay shots of food on wood tables with branded placemats/menus visible. Blue-and-white Vietnamese ceramic bowls are a recurring visual motif.
- **Image carousel on homepage:** 5 sliding food photos (auto-rotating gallery)
- **"Crazy Rich Broth" and "DIY Pho Pot"** appear as branded taglines/features
- **Neon sign:** "Crazy" in yellow neon on white molding wall — used as hero/brand imagery
